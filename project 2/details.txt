# PYTHON FOR DATA ANALYTICS AND BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE 
TOPIC: Python Data Analytics on the E-Commerce Purchases
Dataset Name: E-Commerce Purchases Dataset
Dataset Source: Kaggle - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/utkarsharya/ecommerce-purchases

# OVERVIEW
This project delve into the real-world data analytics on an e-commerce purchases.

To carry out exploratory and detailed analysis, various business questions were developed that addresses the real-world business cases and scenarios. Some of the questions include:

1. Displaying the top 10 rows of the dataset
2. Checking the last 10 rows of the dataset
3. Check Datatype of Each Columns of the dataset
4. Check for Null or Missing values in the dataset
5. How many rows and columns are there in the dataset? 
6. What is the Highest and Lowest Purchase Prices?
7. What is the Average Purchase Price?
8. How many people have French 'fr' as their Language?
9. Job Title Contains Engineer
10. Find The Email of the person with the following IP Address: "132.207.160.22"
11. How many People have Mastercard as their Credit Card Provider and made a purchase above 50?
12. Find the email of the person with the following Credit Card Number: "4664825258997302"
13. How many people purchase during the morning (AM) and how many people purchase during the afternoon (PM)?
14. How many people have a credit card that expires in 2020?
15. What are the top 5 most popular email providers (e.g. gmail.com, yahoo.com, etc...)?
